# Sources for Mogwai (魔怪)

## Primary Classical Chinese Texts

1. **聊齋誌異 (Liaozhai Zhiyi / Strange Tales from a Chinese Studio)** - Pu Songling (17th century)
   - Most famous collection of Chinese supernatural tales
   - Multiple demon and spirit stories
   - Various English translations available

2. **西遊記 (Xiyouji / Journey to the West)** - Wu Cheng'en (16th century)
   - Classic novel featuring numerous demons
   - Buddhist-Daoist demon mythology
   - Multiple scholarly editions and translations

3. **山海經 (Shanhaijing / Classic of Mountains and Seas)** (4th-1st century BCE)
   - Ancient geographical and mythological text
   - Catalog of strange beings and creatures
   - Foundation for later demon lore

4. **搜神記 (Soushenji / In Search of the Supernatural)** - Gan Bao (4th century)
   - Early collection of supernatural tales
   - Proto-demon stories
   - Historical supernatural accounts

5. **太平廣記 (Taiping Guangji / Extensive Records of the Taiping Era)** (10th century)
   - Song dynasty compilation
   - Massive collection including demon stories
   - Encyclopedic supernatural source

## Buddhist Demon Texts

6. **Mara Samyutta** - Pali Canon
   - Buddhist demon king Mara stories
   - Foundation for Chinese demon concepts
   - Available in various translations

7. **華嚴經 (Avatamsaka Sutra / Flower Garland Sutra)**
   - Mahayana Buddhist text
   - Demon realms and beings described
   - Chinese Buddhist canon (大正藏)

8. **楞嚴經 (Śūraṅgama Sūtra)**
   - Important Chinese Buddhist text
   - Detailed demon classifications
   - Meditation obstacles and demons

9. **大智度論 (Mahāprajñāpāramitā-śāstra)** - Nagarjuna
   - Commentary on demons and Mara
   - Buddhist cosmology
   - Chinese translation in Taisho Tripitaka

10. **佛說天地八陽神咒經 (Sutra of Eight Yang Spirit Mantras)**
    - Demon exorcism and protection
    - Apocryphal Chinese Buddhist text
    - Ritual applications

## Daoist Demon Texts

11. **抱朴子 (Baopuzi / The Master Who Embraces Simplicity)** - Ge Hong (4th century)
    - Daoist alchemy and demon lore
    - Protection techniques described
    - Classical Daoist demonology

12. **道藏 (Daozang / Daoist Canon)**
    - Comprehensive Daoist scripture collection
    - Multiple texts on demons and exorcism
    - Various editions and catalogs

13. **太上三五正一盟威籙 (Orthodox Unity Alliance Register)**
    - Daoist ritual text
    - Demon hierarchies and control
    - Celestial bureaucracy

14. **靈寶無量度人上品妙經 (Supreme Scripture of Limitless Salvation)**
    - Daoist liturgical text
    - Salvation of spirits and demons
    - Ritual framework

15. **雷法 (Thunder Magic Texts)** - Song-Yuan dynasties
    - Daoist demon combat techniques
    - Thunder deity invocations
    - Exorcism manuals
